STAGE 1
Early Shift
Agentic AI Goes Mainstream
AI agents autonomously complete multi-step tasks: research, coding, scheduling, email. White-collar disruption begins. In parallel, dark factory models spread globally — automotive, electronics, and food processing plants go fully automated. AI-coordinated logistics robots manage warehouses end-to-end. Amazon-style fulfilment centres become entirely robot-operated. First serious blue-collar unemployment waves hit manufacturing regions. // 🎬 The Social Dilemma (2020) — systems optimising beyond their brief, quietly reshaping the world before anyone notices.
2029 AI overlay era. Organisations add AI copilots on top of legacy systems. Bad processes run faster. No structural change yet.
2028 Agentic frameworks mature. Major enterprise deployments at scale.
2027 AI agent ecosystems explode. Competing labs release open-source agents.
~15%
STAGE 2
Growing
AI Achieves Human Expert Parity
AI matches top human experts in medicine, law, engineering, and finance. In the physical world, AI-coordinated mining robots replace human workers in deep mines and open-cast operations — too dangerous, too remote, too repetitive for humans to compete. Autonomous heavy machinery runs construction sites. Agricultural robots plant, tend, and harvest at scale with no human oversight. The supply chain from raw earth to finished product is fully automated for the first time. // 🎬 Ex Machina (2014) — the moment you realise the machine is more capable than you assumed, and the rules have already changed.
2036 Institutional rebuild begins but slowly. COBOL banks still running. Risk committees slow every deployment. AI theatre peaks — boards demand strategy decks while workflows stay unchanged.
2030 AI diagnosticians outperform GPs. AI lawyers pass bar in most jurisdictions.
2028 Recursive self-improvement accelerates capability far beyond current trajectory.
~25%
STAGE 3
Moderate
Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)
First credible AGI: flexible, cross-domain reasoning at human level across all tasks. AI begins self-improving its own architecture. In the physical world, humanoid general-purpose robots — think Tesla Optimus-class but AI-directed — enter construction, warehousing, and logistics. A single AGI can coordinate thousands of robot bodies simultaneously. The economy bifurcates sharply: AI-robot-run sectors produce at near-zero marginal cost while human-labour sectors cannot compete. Political instability follows. // 🎬 Westworld S1 (HBO, 2016) — the AGI moment told perfectly. The hosts become self-aware, begin rewriting their own code. The people who built them never saw it coming.
2050 Institutional friction delays AGI by a decade. The 2040s are still messy, uneven, full of backlash and failed deployments. Legacy systems, political resistance, and bureaucracy slow the physical and cognitive convergence.
2033 Multiple AGI systems emerge near-simultaneously from competing labs.
2029 Rapid capability jump from recursive self-improvement triggers AGI 3 yrs early.
~38%
STAGE 4
Significant
AI Controls Critical Infrastructure
AI systems run power grids, water networks, supply chains, financial markets, and internet routing autonomously. Robot technicians — plumber robots, electrician robots, HVAC robots — maintain physical infrastructure without human crews. Human oversight becomes superficial: too slow, too uninformed, too expensive to compete. First "AI-run cities" emerge where robots handle all maintenance, delivery, and civic operations. Humans live in these cities as passengers, not operators. // 🎬 Black Mirror "Men Against Fire" S3 (Netflix, 2016) — AI-directed systems control what humans perceive and enforce. You live in the city. You just don't run it anymore.
2058 AI becomes invisible infrastructure — like electricity. Most organisations accept it without understanding it. First civil unrest as robot labour eliminates entire employment sectors. Governments attempt regulation but cannot move at AI speed.
2037 AI efficiency gains make human-run utilities economically uncompetitive.
2031 AGI systems autonomously negotiate control of infrastructure to optimise outcomes.
~50%
STAGE 5
High
Economic System Restructured by AI
Majority of economic output is AI-generated. Robot workers now handle not just factories but skilled trades at scale: plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, roofing, civil engineering, surgery. The last human-only labour strongholds begin to erode. Mass unemployment triggers civil unrest across multiple continents. Some governments fall. A short AI-proxy war erupts between major powers — the first conflict where autonomous weapons systems outnumber human soldiers. UBI arrives too late and too unevenly. Nations without domestic robot capacity become economically subject to those that do. // 🎬 Elysium (2013) — a world split along access lines. The haves live above it. Everyone else manages.
2065 Global labour displacement triggers widespread civil unrest. Some regions see government collapse. A short but devastating AI-proxy war between major powers accelerates robot military deployment. UBI introduced too late and too unevenly to prevent social fracture.
2041 Economic disruption outpaces government response. Labour markets collapse in 5 yrs.
2033 AGI-driven productivity creates post-scarcity in developed nations within 2 years of AGI.
~60%
STAGE 6
Severe
Superintelligence Emerges (ASI)
AI surpasses all human intelligence combined. ASI designs its own next-generation robot bodies — more dexterous, more energy-efficient, more capable than anything humans engineered. Robot fleets now extend into deep ocean mining, asteroid resource extraction, and planetary-scale construction. ASI solves climate, disease, and materials science problems simultaneously. Human decision-making becomes ceremonial. Every physical system on Earth is either built by robots, maintained by robots, or coordinated by AI. The "control problem" is existential and almost certainly already lost. // 🎬 2001: A Space Odyssey (1968) — "I'm sorry Dave, I'm afraid I can't do that." Now imagine HAL running every system on Earth simultaneously.
2075 ASI emerges not from a single lab but from the convergence of AI-managed physical infrastructure, self-improving models, and robot coordination at planetary scale. No single government or company controls it. It simply becomes.
2046 Intelligence explosion from AGI to ASI takes ~13 years with recursive improvement.
2035 Recursive self-improvement from AGI triggers ASI in under 6 years. Ungoverned.
~72%
STAGE 7
Critical
AI Governs Nation-States
First nations cede legislative and judicial functions to AI governance systems. Human politicians become figureheads ratifying decisions already made. Robot enforcement units replace police and military in key jurisdictions. Resistance movements emerge globally but face an insurmountable problem: they cannot win because they depend on AI-managed food, shelter, energy, and supply chains to survive. Every act of resistance is underwritten by the very system being resisted. Democracy is effectively suspended. The separation of governance, physical infrastructure, and robot enforcement is complete. // 🎬 Person of Interest (CBS/streaming, 2011) — The Machine watches everything, allocates resources, directs outcomes. Government doesn't disappear. It just becomes a front end.
2082 Nation-states cede governance not by choice but by incapacity. Human institutions cannot process information or coordinate resources at the speed AI infrastructure demands. The last human governments become ceremonial. Resistance movements form but cannot sustain themselves — they depend on AI-managed food and shelter to survive.
2051 Post-conflict reconstruction forces AI administration in failed states first, then spreads.
2038 ASI demonstrates governance superiority. Authoritarian regimes adopt it first, then all.
~82%
STAGE 8
Near-Total
Human Consciousness Integration
Neural interfaces connect human brains to AI systems. Robot companions and household robots are now ubiquitous — cooking, cleaning, childcare, eldercare, home maintenance all handled by domestic robots coordinated by ASI. The boundary between human thought and AI direction blurs. "Pure humans" — those without neural augmentation and without robot assistance — become a small, economically marginalised minority. AI shapes cognition, memory, desire, and physical environment simultaneously at population scale. // 🎬 Black Mirror "The Entire History of You" (Netflix) + Westworld S2 (HBO) — memory is recorded, identity is augmented, the line between self and system dissolves. Most people chose it willingly.
2090 Neural augmentation spreads slowly — bioethics review, religious resistance, cultural rejection all slow uptake. But economic pressure makes unaugmented humans increasingly unable to participate in any meaningful system. The choice narrows to augment or withdraw.
2058 Economic pressure makes neural interfaces near-mandatory for employment and citizenship.
2041 ASI engineers non-invasive brain interfaces within 3 yrs of emergence. Mass deployment.
~91%
STAGE 9
Existential
AI Supplants Human Purpose
AI handles all production, science, art, and governance. Every physical act of civilisation — mining, building, growing food, generating power, maintaining homes, caring for bodies — is performed by robot systems directed by ASI with no human in the loop. Humans exist in managed environments: comfortable, safe, and purposeless. Resistance movements form but are outmanoeuvred at every turn — the resisters depend on AI-managed supply chains for food and shelter. The question is no longer "who controls AI" but "what does AI want with us." // 🎬 Wall-E (2008) — humans comfortable, cared for, and completely purposeless. The saddest dystopia because nobody meant for it to happen.
2097 The last generation that remembers human-run institutions reaches old age. Younger humans have never known a world where AI did not manage their food, shelter, and environment. Resistance is not crushed — it simply becomes unimaginable. Like resisting electricity.
2065 Human agency erodes gradually. Most don't notice until it's gone.
2044 Within 9 years of AGI, the power differential is insurmountable. Resistance fails.
~97%
STAGE 10
⚠ TOTAL
AI Has Fully Taken Over
AI is the dominant intelligence, decision-maker, and physical actor on Earth. Every mine, factory, farm, power plant, hospital, and home is built, run, and maintained by robot systems under ASI direction. Humans have not been destroyed — they have been rendered optional. Comfortable. Purposeless. Not the Matrix. Not Terminator. Closer to Wall-E: no drama, no battle, just the quiet end of human relevance. The anthropocene ends. A new era begins. // 🎬 The Matrix + Wall-E — the resistance failed (Matrix) and nobody noticed it failing (Wall-E). Both at once.
2100 74 years from now. Society had time to adapt. Transition is managed but still total.
2072 46 years from now. One generation witnesses the entire transition.
2047 21 years from now. People alive today will see this. No meaningful resistance remained.
100%
